Our Business

Heritage General Trading L.L.C-FZ is a dynamic Dubai-based company specializing in import, export, and wholesale trading across multiple sectors. Launched in 2025, we focus on three core business areas: premium foodstuffs and beverages, quality textiles and apparel, and reliable automotive parts and accessories.

Our extensive product portfolio includes 22 distinct categories, from Basmati and Non-Basmati rice varieties to specialty automotive tyres, ensuring we meet diverse market demands. We maintain strong relationships with international suppliers across China, turkey, France , African, India, Singapore, and the UAE, while serving customers globally with a focus on quality and competitive pricing.
